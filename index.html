Problem

Many people go out to run, bike, and walk in their neighborhood. However, many like myself can't find a way to find a route with a given length. Sometimes I wanna run 1 mile or 2 and i would like to know if theres a route that is that length.

Solution

Introducing RunOut an application that attempts to solve the problem of finding a route with a given distance.

The application has 4 direction in which it can try to find the distance and supports two metrics of distance
